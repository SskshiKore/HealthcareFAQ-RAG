Fever is a temporary rise in body temperature. Treatment includes rest, hydration and paracetamol.
Cold is a viral infection causing runny nose and sneezing. Treatment includes steam inhalation and fluids.
Headache is often caused by stress or dehydration. Treatment includes rest and hydration.
Diabetes is a chronic disease affecting blood sugar. Managed by insulin, diet and exercise.
Hypertension means high blood pressure. Managed by lifestyle changes and medication.
Asthma causes breathing difficulty due to airway inflammation. Treated using inhalers.
Back pain occurs due to posture or strain. Managed with physiotherapy and rest.
Allergy is an immune reaction causing sneezing and itching. Antihistamines help.
COVID-19 is a respiratory viral disease. Vaccination is recommended.
Stomach pain may be caused by gas or infection. Use antacids and hydration.
